## Hypothesis

Families would pay ~₹300–800/mo for one app replacing twelve.

## Evidence

Indian consumers overwhelmingly don't pay for utility-app subscriptions; my own
plan was US-framed while my market was Indian households.

## Result

Pivoted to free-for-families with B2B/ads revenue; re-prioritized the roadmap
around partner-relevant moments of intent.

## What I'd do differently

Pressure-test the revenue model against the actual target market before writing
40 pages around the wrong one.

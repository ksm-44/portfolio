## Hypothesis

Chat-based manual capture, if under 10 seconds, beats error-prone automation.

## Evidence

Auto-import scoping showed each integration was slow to build and broke
silently; missed cash/split transactions are exactly what makes balances drift
and kills trust.

## Result

1,000+ transactions logged in 2 months with zero balance drift; cash and splits
actually get captured.

## What I'd do differently

Kill the auto-import option a week earlier. I scoped it longer than the
evidence justified.
